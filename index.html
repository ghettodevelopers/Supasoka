Privacy Policy for FlexFit

Last Updated: January 2, 2026

1. Introduction

FlexFit ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our health and fitness mobile application.

IMPORTANT: FlexFit is an educational health and wellness app. It does NOT provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical concerns.

2. Information We Collect

FlexFit collects the following data types, which are stored locally on your device only:

Health & Fitness Data (Stored Locally)

  • Step Count: Daily step tracking using your device's motion sensors (ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION permission)
  • Water Intake: Your logged water consumption to help you stay hydrated
  • Workout History: Exercise sessions, duration, and types of workouts completed
  • Activity Logs: Records of your fitness activities and progress
  • Personal Goals: Your fitness and wellness goals and preferences

Device & App Activity

  • App Usage: Features you use and interact with
  • Notifications: Reminders and motivational messages (if you enable them)

3. How We Use Your Information

All health and fitness data collected by FlexFit is:

  • Stored locally on your device only - not transmitted to any servers
  • Used solely for app functionality (tracking your progress, showing statistics, providing personalized features)
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Not used for marketing or analytics purposes

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Health Data: NOT SHARED

Your health and fitness data is NEVER shared with anyone. It remains on your device and under your control.

Third-Party Services We Use:

  • Google AdMob: We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device and usage information for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy

Important: AdMob does NOT have access to your health and fitness data (steps, workouts, water intake, etc.).

5. Permissions Explained

ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION

Purpose: To count your daily steps using your device's motion sensors

Usage: Enables step tracking feature for fitness monitoring

Optional: You can use other app features without granting this permission

INTERNET & NETWORK_STATE

Purpose: To display advertisements and check connectivity

POST_NOTIFICATIONS

Purpose: To send workout reminders and motivational messages

Optional: You can disable notifications in device settings

6. Data Security

  • All health data is stored locally on your device using secure Android storage
  • Data is protected by your device's security features (screen lock, encryption)
  • We do not transmit health data over the internet
  • No server-side storage of personal health information

7. Your Rights and Choices

Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  • Uninstall the app - All locally stored data will be permanently removed
  • Clear app data - Go to device Settings > Apps > FlexFit > Storage > Clear Data

Permission Management

You can revoke permissions at any time in your device settings (Settings > Apps > FlexFit > Permissions)

8. Children's Privacy

FlexFit is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Medical Disclaimer

FlexFit provides educational content only:

  • Kegel exercises and fitness tips are for educational purposes
  • Step counting and activity tracking are for personal fitness monitoring
  • Nutrition and hydration guidance is general wellness information
  • This app does NOT diagnose, treat, or prevent any medical condition
  • Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for medical advice

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page. Continued use of FlexFit after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
